HR Prop Framework

Daily Top Picks Analytics — minimum 2 signals before any bet

Stack Vulnerable Pitchers

Use Lineups tab. Target pitchers where 2+ batters score ≥ 70. Stack teammates vs same starter.

Elite Thresholds

🎯 Elite: .300 ISO / .500 SLG / 92+ EV / 20%+ Barrel
✅ Target: .200 ISO / .400 SLG / 89+ EV / 12%+ Barrel

Park + Weather Stack

Matchup ≥ 70 + park ≥ 1.15x + wind out = triple signal. Best plays of the day.

2-3 Leg Parlays Only

1 elite + 1 value vs same pitcher. Or 2 teammates vs vulnerable starter. Never more than 3 legs.

Pitcher HR Allowed Props

Over 1.5 HR Allowed lines — great value when 3+ batters show ≥ 70 vs same starter in hitter park.

Velocity Drops = Edge

Pitcher down 2+ mph from season avg = significant HR risk increase regardless of ERA. Always check Velo Trend column.

Signal Checklist — Need 2+ Before Betting
✅ Barrel% ≥ 12% Favorable / ≥ 20% Elite
✅ Exit Velo ≥ 89 mph / ≥ 92 mph Elite
✅ HR Risk vs batter hand ≥ 1.5
✅ Pitcher Barrel% Against ≥ 7%
✅ Platoon advantage confirmed
✅ Park factor ≥ 1.10x
✅ Matchup score ≥ 70
✅ Batter in preferred day/night slot

🔥 Form Tags (Last 14 Days)
🔥 On Fire — AVG .040+ above season avg, multiple recent HRs. Strongest buy signal.
🔥 Hot — AVG .020+ above season avg. Batter trending up.
→ Neutral — Consistent with season average. No edge either way.
❄ Cold — AVG .020+ below season avg. Avoid or reduce units.
❄ Ice Cold — AVG .040+ below season avg. Fade strongly.
⚾ Pitcher Stats
HR Risk RHB / LHB
Home runs allowed per 9 innings split by batter handedness. ≥1.5 = target. ≥1.8 = ideal.
HR/9
Total HR allowed per 9 innings regardless of handedness.
SwStr%
Swinging strike rate. ≥18% = elite whiff pitcher, hard to hit HRs. ≤11% = hittable.
Velo Trend
Recent velocity vs season average. Down 1+ mph = elevated HR risk regardless of ERA.
Barrel% Against
How often batters barrel the ball vs this pitcher. ≥10% = elite danger, ≥7% = vulnerable.
🏓 Batter Stats
Barrel%
% of batted balls that are barreled (optimal launch angle + exit velo). ≥20% = elite. ≥12% = favorable.
EV (Exit Velocity)
Average speed off the bat in mph. ≥92 = elite hard contact. ≥89 = favorable.
ISO
Isolated power (SLG minus AVG). Pure power measurement. ≥.250 = elite. ≥.200 = favorable.
wOBA
Weighted on-base average. Best single hitting metric. ≥.370 = above average. ≥.400 = elite.
Platoon Advantage
LHB vs RHP or RHB vs LHP. Batters hit significantly better against opposite-hand pitchers.
🏟 Park Factors
🔥 1.15x+ — Strongly HR friendly. Coors Field (1.35x), GABP (1.28x), Yankee Stadium (1.22x).
✅ 1.05-1.15x — Slightly HR friendly. Minor boost.
⚪ ~1.00x — Neutral park. No significant HR effect.
❌ <0.90x — Pitcher friendly. Avoid HR props here. Oracle Park (0.72x), loanDepot (0.78x).
🌡 Weather Signals
Wind OUT 15+ mph — Blowing toward outfield. Significant HR boost. Best weather condition.
Wind OUT 8+ mph — Mild HR boost. Minor positive factor.
Wind IN 15+ mph — Blowing toward infield. Strong HR suppressor. Avoid props.
80°F+ — Warm air is thinner, ball carries further. Minor positive.
🏠 Dome — Indoor stadium. Weather completely irrelevant.
📊 Matchup Score
Composite score 0-100 for each batter vs pitcher matchup. Combines platoon advantage, HR Risk split, Barrel%, Exit Velo, ISO, K%, SwStr%, velocity trend, and recent form.
70-100 — GOOD
Strong HR prop target. Multiple signals aligned.
55-69 — NEUTRAL
Some signals present. Use as parlay leg only.
<55 — RISKY/AVOID
Signals point against batter. Skip this pick.
🎯 Model Odds & Edge%
Model Odds — Our estimated fair price based on HR probability from matchup score. e.g. +420 means our model thinks the fair line is +420.
Positive Edge% — Market is paying more than model says it should. Value bet — the line is in your favor.
Negative Edge% — Market has this priced tighter than model. Overpriced — skip or reduce units.
📋 The 2-Signal Rule
Never bet a HR prop on a single signal alone. Always require at least 2 of the following before placing a bet:
✅ Matchup score ≥70    ✅ Barrel% ≥12%    ✅ HR Risk vs hand ≥1.5    ✅ Park factor ≥1.10x    ✅ Wind blowing out    ✅ Batter hot last 14 days    ✅ Pitcher velo drop 1+ mph
